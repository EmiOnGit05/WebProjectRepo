Welcome to your first step of becoming a cat owner!

Everyone has a different story for how they got their cat. I got my four of my cats from a friend, who's cat had kittens. I got my other cat, Milo, bought from PetSmart. Many people find the cats outside, and sometimes, the cats find them! This page is going to give a few trusted examples of adoption centers, that take care of these cats with kindness and care! If you have any additional questions, please refer to the 'Ask Dev' page and submit a form!
