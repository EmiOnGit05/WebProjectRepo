Picking the Right Litter

Cats need a clean place to "go to the bathroom". Litter boxes need to be cleaned out regularly, sometimes once or twice a day depending on how many cats you have. These are some suggestions for litter boxes, both automatic and regular. WARNING: Please do research when buying a robotic litter box. Make sure that they are either manually activated to clean, or stop when something is in the way to prevent any bad things happening to your kitty!

Keeping Your Cat's Fur Healthy

Grooming is not super important with cats like it is with many other animals. Cats like to groom themselves for the most part, but brushing can still really help with their overall health. Hairy cats can get a lot of hairballs and throw up from grooming, so brushing them can help reduce the amount of fur they ingest to something they can handle. This also helps them be softer and lighter!
